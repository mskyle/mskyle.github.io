An Exciting Day

Aug 31st, 2013

Not So Good Morning

So today I decided I would get stuff done early, and at 8AM I was out the door. By 9AM I’d been to Target, Trader Joe’s, and Whole Foods (because I’m a ridiculous parody of myself) which are way more pleasant to visit at 8AM than at normal times of day. I was really proud of myself. Self-satisfied, even!

Then I came home and immediately locked myself out. My roommate was in Maine and I didn’t know who else had keys (I’m only staying there temporarily and haven’t exactly got the full lay of the land yet). There was frozen food in the car. It was not a great situation. The house is a duplex and I was only locked out of the front door, not inner door, but the other owner had just left and I had no idea when she’d be back, nor do I have her phone number.

Then I remembered that other owner is always leaving her doors open, which I know because sometimes her cat wanders downstairs. So I thought, what the hell. How often do you have a good excuse to wander through a complete stranger’s home? Moments later I was letting myself in the back door and sneaking through her apartment (I have not yet met this woman) and down the front stairs, where I was reunited with my keys, wallet, and other sundry belongings (to say nothing of the frozen vegetables).

Then I let the cat escape up the stairs (this would be a cat who is supposed to live in my apartment, not the cat who lives in the upstairs apartment) and once again had to go upstairs to the other apartment to retrieve the cat. It was 9:30 and I already needed a nap.

A Relaxing Afternoon

Probably I should have just taken a nap at that point but instead I got down to work on my Breakable Toy project for Launch Academy. I’m working on a web app that will let you record your hikes (dates, photos, etc.) and share them with other people. I’m getting very attached to it!
